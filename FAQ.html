Frequent Ask Questions Regarding X-Trade

X-Trade is an online trading platform which allows all Xiamen University's staffs and student to trade their products.

Membership of X-Trade is exclusive for students and staffs of Xiamen University and Xiamen University Malaysia.  If you are one of them, you can sign-up and become a membership of X-Trade.

After being a member of X-Trade, you are able to upload your product to X-Trade. After being approved by our officer, you product will be uploaded to the product page and view by others. You can start trading after that. YAY!
